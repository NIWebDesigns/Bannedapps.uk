Guide to Installing IPTV Apps on Windows

Follow the steps below to download, install, and set up IPTV apps on your Windows device.

Step 1: Download the App

1. Open your preferred web browser on your Windows device.

2. Go to the following link to download the IPTV app: iptvsmarters.com.

Step 2: Install the App

1. Locate the downloaded file, typically in your Downloads folder.

2. Double-click the downloaded file to start the installation process.

3. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation.

Step 3: Open the App

1. Once the installation is complete, find the app in your Start Menu.

2. Click on the app icon to open it.

Step 4: Set Up Your Account

1. You will see a welcome screen. Click OK to proceed.

2. Enter your Username and Password as provided by your IPTV service.

3. Tap Login to access the app.
