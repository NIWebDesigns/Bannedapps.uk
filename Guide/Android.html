Guide to Installing IPTV Apps on Android Devices

Follow the steps below to download, install, and set up IPTV apps on your Android device.

Step 1: Download the App

1. Open your preferred web browser on your Android device.

2. Go to the following link to download the app: tinyurl.com/tvsv3.

Step 2: Enable Unknown Sources

1. Go to Settings on your Android device.

2. Scroll down and tap on Security (or Privacy on some devices).

3. Find Install Unknown Apps and select the browser you used to download the app.

4. Enable the option to allow installations from this source.

Step 3: Install the App

1. Locate the downloaded APK file in your device's Downloads folder.

2. Tap on the APK file to begin the installation process.

3. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation.

Step 4: Open the App

1. Once the installation is complete, find the app in your app drawer.

2. Tap the app icon to open it.

Step 5: Set Up Your Account

1. You will see a welcome screen. Click OK to proceed.

2. Enter your Username and Password as provided by your IPTV service.

3. Tap Login to access the app.
