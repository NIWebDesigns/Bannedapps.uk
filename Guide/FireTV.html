Guide to Installing IPTV Apps on Firestick

If you are uninstalling and reinstalling, skip to Step 4.

Follow these steps to enable third-party apps on your Amazon Firestick, allowing you to access IPTV services.

Step 1: Download the Downloader App

From the Firestick home screen, go to the Search icon and search for Downloader.

You can also hold the Alexa button on your Firestick remote and say "Downloader" to bring up the app and install it this way.<\/p>

Select Downloader from the results and click on Download to install the app.

Step 2: Enable Developer Options

Go to Settings on the Firestick home screen.

Select My Fire TV and then About.

Scroll down to Fire TV Stick and tap it five times until you see a message that says "You are now a developer."

Step 3: Allow Apps from Unknown Sources

Return to the My Fire TV menu and select Developer Options.

Click on Install Unknown Apps and enable it specifically for the Downloader app.

Step 4: Open Downloader and Enter URL

Open the Downloader app from your home screen.

In the Downloader app, enter the URL bannedapps.uk to access and download IPTV apps.

On the website, locate the first section and download the IPTV apps available.

Step 5: Install the IPTV Apps

Once downloaded, you’ll be prompted to install each app. Select Install for each app.

After installation, you can open the apps directly or find them in your app library under Apps.

You’re All Set!

Enjoy using the IPTV apps on your Firestick!
