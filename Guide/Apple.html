Guide to Installing IPTV Apps on iPhone

Follow the steps below to download, install, and set up your preferred IPTV app on your iPhone.

Option 1: Install Purple Playlist Player

Follow these steps to download and set up Purple Playlist Player:

Steps to Download and Set Up

  1. Open the App Store on your iPhone.
  2. Tap on the Search icon at the bottom of the screen.
  3. In the search bar, type Purple Playlist Player and press Search.
  4. Locate the app in the search results and tap Get to download it.
  5. Once downloaded, tap Open to launch the app.
  6. Select Login With Code.
  7. Enter Access Code: 79KNSW.
  8. Select Server - frequency.
  9. Enter your Username & Password.
  10. Select Remember Me and tap Login.

Option 2: Install Smart IPTV

Follow these steps to download and set up Smart IPTV:

Steps to Download and Set Up

  1. Open the App Store on your iPhone.
  2. Tap on the Search icon at the bottom of the screen.
  3. In the search bar, type Smart IPTV and press Search.
  4. Locate the app in the search results and tap Get to download it.
  5. Once downloaded, tap Open to launch the app.
  6. Select Login with Xtream Codes API.
  7. Enter the server URL, your Username, and Password.
  8. Tap Add User to log in.
